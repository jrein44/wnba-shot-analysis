üîç Key Findings

Finding #1: Stewart Thrives in Clutch

+8.8% better in clutch situations (54.1% FG vs 45.3% rest of game)

Despite this elite performance, she only receives 18.4% of clutch possessions. Increasing her usage in critical moments could significantly improve close-game outcomes.

Finding #2: The Sabrina Paradox

Handles 39.3% of clutch possessions but efficiency drops -5.5%

High usage with contested 3PT attempts (49.4% of clutch shots) leads to lower efficiency. She shoots only 29.9% from three overall but is more efficient on 2PT attempts (49.3%).

Recommendation: Reduce contested 3PT attempts, increase drives for higher-percentage looks.

Finding #3: Jones is Underutilized

Elite efficiency: 49.0% FG and 42.4% from 3PT

Only 13.4% clutch usage despite being 8.6 percentage points above league average (33.8%) from three. Her efficiency actually INCREASES in Q4 (57.4%).

Recommendation: Design specific plays for Jones in final possessions.
